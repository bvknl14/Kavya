ABOUT US

KAVYA Group is a globally recognized research and technology organization headquartered in the United Kingdom. With over a century of expertise, this group collaborates with businesses across the footwear, leather, and associated industries to drive innovation, quality, and sustainability.

Our Mission At KAVYA Group, our mission is to advance standards and performance in footwear and leather goods through rigorous research, testing, and certification. We are committed to supporting our partners worldwide in achieving excellence and sustainability in their products.

Core Services: - Research & Development: Pioneering research into materials, manufacturing processes, and product performance. - Testing & Certification: Independent testing and certification services to ensure compliance with international standards. - Consultancy: Expert advice and support on product development, quality assurance, and manufacturing efficiency. - Training: Industry-leading training programs to enhance skills and knowledge across the supply chain.

Industries We Serve: KAVYA Group works with a wide range of industries, including footwear manufacturers, leather goods producers, retailers, and raw material suppliers. Our insights and services cater to businesses of all sizes, from multinational corporations to small-scale enterprises.
